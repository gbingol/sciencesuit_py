pbinom

Computes the left-tailed cumulative probability values.
Returns number/Vector/Array.

Syntax:

1) pbinom{q=, size=, prob=}

2) pbinom(q=, size=, prob=)

q: Quantile ← number/Vector/Array
size: Number of independent trials ← integer
prob: Probability of success on each trial [0,1] ← number

 

Example Usage:

>>std.pbinom{q=2,size=5, prob=0.3}
0.83692
