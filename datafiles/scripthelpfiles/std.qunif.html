qunif

Computes the lower tail quantile.
Returns number/Vector/Array.

Syntax:

1) qunif {p=, min=0, max=1}

2) qunif (p=,min=0, max=1)

 

p: Cumulative probability ← number/Vector/Array
min, max: Lower and upper limits of the distribution ← number

Example Usage:

>>std.qunif{p=0.5, min=1, max=5}
3
