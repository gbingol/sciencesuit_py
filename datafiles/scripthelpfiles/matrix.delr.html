Matrix:delr

Deletes a row at the specified position, if the matrix will have at least 2 rows after the deletion.

Syntax

delr (pos=)

 

Example:

>>m=std.util.tomatrix{ {1,2,3} , {4,5,6} , {7,8,9} }

>>m:delr(2)
>>m
1    2    3
7    8    9

Alternatively,m[2]=nil would have given the same result.
