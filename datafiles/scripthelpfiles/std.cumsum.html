cumsum

Finds the cumulative sum of a Vector, Matrix or other iteratable containers.

cumsum(Vector/Array) → Vector/Array

cumsum(Matrix, [axes=]) → Matrix

cumsum(Container) → table

 

Vector

>>v=std.util.tovector{1, 2, 3, 4}
>>v
1   2  3  4  COL

>>std.cumsum(v)
1  3  6  10  COL

 

Matrix

>>m=std.util.tomatrix{ {1, 2}, {3, 4} }
>>m
1   2
3   4

>>std.cumsum(m)
1   3
6   10

>>std.cumsum(m, 0)
1   2
4   6

>>std.cumsum(m, 1)
1   3
3   7

 

Lua table

>>t={1, 2, 3, 4}
>>t
1  2  3  4

>>std.cumsum(t)
1  3  6  10


>>t2={a=1, b=2, 3, 4, 5}

--skipping keys of type string
>>std.cumsum(t2)
3  7  12

 

Array

>>arr
1  2 "A"  "B" "D" 3

--string entries are skipped
>>std.cumsum(arr)
1  3  6
