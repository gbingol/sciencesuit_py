rchisq

Generates n ≥1 random numbers from the chi-square distribution.
Returns a Vector or a number.

Syntax:

1) rchisq{n=, df=}

2) rchisq(n=, df=)

 

n: Number of random numbers ← integer
df: Degrees of freedom ← integer

Example:

>>std.rchisq{n=5, df=5}
6.38474    8.02805    2.90747    3.27364    2.33255    COL
