sum

Sums up addable entries in an iteratable container.

Syntax:

sum(container=) → entry

returns sum of elements of type entry in the container.

 

Example:

>>t={3, 5, 2, 1, 7, 4}
 
>>std.sum(t)
22


>>v=std.util.tovector(t)
>>v
3    5    2    1    7    4    COL

>>std.sum(v)
22


>>m=std.util.tomatrix{ {1, 2} , {3, 4} }
>>m
1    2
3    4

>>std.sum(m)
10
