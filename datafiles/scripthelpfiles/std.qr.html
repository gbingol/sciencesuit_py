QR

Finds QR decomposition of a given matrix.

Syntax:

qr(A)

 

Example:

>>m=std.util.tomatrix{{10, -7, 0}, {-3, 2, 6} , {5, -1, 5} }
>>m
10 -7 0
-3 2 6
5 -1 5

>>q,r=std.qr(m)
>>q
-0.86387 0.42560 -0.26943
0.25916 -0.08313 -0.96225
-0.43193 -0.90108 -0.03849

>>r
-11.57584 6.99733 -0.60471
0.00000 -2.24440 -5.00417
0.00000 0.00000 -5.96595
