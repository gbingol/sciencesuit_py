Food:rho

Calculates the density (kg/m3) of the food material

Syntax:

rho(arg) → number

Notes:

  1. If arg is not provided, then predictive equations will be used.
  2. If arg is a number, then ρ=num regardless of composition.
  3. If arg is a function, then ρ=func(food)

Example:

>>f=Food.new{Water=30, CHO=70}

>>f:rho()
1413.75

>>f:rho(2000)
>>f:rho()
2000
