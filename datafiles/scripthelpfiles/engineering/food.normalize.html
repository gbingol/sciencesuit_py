Food:normalize

Only sets the weight to 1.0.

Note:

Syntax:

normalize()

Example:

>>milk=Food.new{water=88.13, protein=3.15, CHO=4.80, lipid=3.25, ash=0.67}
>>water=Food.new{water=100}

>>powder=milk-0.87*water
>>powder
Weight (unit weight)=0.13

This is a rather important function as the food material that is a result of an
addition or a subtraction operation can have a weight different than 1.0.

For example: if you were to use the variable powder as is in another mathematical operation,
its weight would have been considered as 0.13 units.
