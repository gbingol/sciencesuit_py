Food:new

Creates a new food material.

Syntax:

new{water=, CHO=, protein=, lipid= (oil/fat), ash=, salt=}

The values of the tables keys are percentages and must add up to 100.
Otherwise it will be scaled to 100.

Example:

>>milk=Food.new{water=88.13, protein=3.15, CHO=4.80, lipid=3.25, ash=0.67}

>> food=Food.new{CHO=30, protein=70}
