Food:cp

Calculates specific heat capacity (kJ/kgK) of the food material

Syntax:

cp(arg) → number

Notes:

  1. If arg is not provided, then predictive equations will be used.
  2. If arg is a number, then cp=num regardless of composition.
  3. If arg is a function, then cp=func(food)

Example:

>>f=Food.new{Water=30, CHO=70}

>>f:cp()
2.34875

>>f:cp(3.0)
>>f:cp()
3
