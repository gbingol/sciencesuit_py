Food:k

Calculates the thermal conductivity (W/mK) of the food material.

Syntax:

k(arg) → number

Notes:

  1. If arg is not provided, then predictive equations will be used.
  2. If arg is a number, then k=num regardless of composition.
  3. If arg is a function, then k=func(food)

Example:

>>myfood=Food.new{Water=30, CHO=70}
>>myfood:k()
0.306101

>>myfood:k(0.20)
>>myfood:k()
0.2
