qbinom

Computes the lower tail quantile (inverse of pbinom).
Returns number/Vector/Array.

Syntax:

1) qbinom{p=, size=, prob=}

2) qbinom(p=, size=, prob=)

 

p: Cumulative probability (left-tailed) ← number/Vector/Array
size: Number of independent trials ← integer
prob: Probability of success on each trial [0,1] ← number

Example Usage:

>>std.qbinom{p=0.83692, size=5, prob=0.3}
2
