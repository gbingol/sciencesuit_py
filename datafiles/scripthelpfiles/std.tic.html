tic

Creates and starts the timer.

Syntax:

tic()

 

The timer is started with the tic() function and is stopped with the toc() function.

 

Example:

>>m1=std.rand(1000, 1000)
>>m2=std.rand(1000, 1000)

--start the timer and immediately multiply
>>std.tic(); m=m1*m2; std.toc()

Elapsed time: 0.501284 seconds.
