quantile

Computes the quantile of an iteratable container.

Syntax:

quantile(x=, prob=) → number
quantile{x=, prob=} → number

 

Example:

>>t={3, 5, 2, 1, 7, 4}

>>std.quantile{x=t, prob=0}
1
>>std.quantile{x=t, prob=0.25}
2.25
>>std.quantile{x=t, prob=0.5}
3.5
>>std.quantile{x=t, prob=0.75}
4.75
>>std.quantile{x=t, prob=1}
7
