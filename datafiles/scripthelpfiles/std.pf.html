pf

Computes the cumulative (left-tailed) probability .
Returns number/Vector/Array.

Syntax:

1) pf{q=, df1=, df2=}

2) pf(q=, df1=, df2=)

 

q: Quantile ← number/Vector/Array
df1, df2: Degrees of freedom ← integer

Example:

>>std.pf{q=1, df1=3, df2=5}
0.535145

>>std.pf{q=2, df1=3, df2=5}
0.767376
