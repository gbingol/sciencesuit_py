rf

Generates random sample for the F distribution.
Returns a Vector or a number.

Syntax:

1) rf{n=, df1=, df2=}

2) rf(n=, df1=, df2=)

 

n: Number of random numbers ← integer
df: Degrees of freedom ← integer

Example:

>>std.rf{n=5, df1=3, df2=5}
1.62134    1.76374    3.38552    0.771544    1.32867    COL
