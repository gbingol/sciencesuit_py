max

Uses minmax to find the maximum of an iteratable container.

max(container) → entry

max(Matrix, axes=) → Vector

>>v=std.util.tovector{3, 5, 2, 1, 7, 4}
>>v
3    5    2    1    7    4    COL

>>std.max(v)
7


>>m

2     4     3     COL
