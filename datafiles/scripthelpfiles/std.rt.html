rt

Generates random sample for the Student t distribution.
Returns a Vector or a number.

Syntax:

1) rt{n=, df=}

2) rt(n=, df=)

 

n: Number of random numbers ← integer
df: Degrees of freedom ← integer

Example:

>>std.rt{n=5, df=5}
-1.0581    0.0325742    -0.980538    1.71746    -0.593335    COL
