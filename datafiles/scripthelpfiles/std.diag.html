diag

Finds the diagonal entries of a matrix.
Returns a Vector.

Syntax:

diag(m=)

Example:

>>m=std.util.tomatrix{ {1, 2, 3} , {4, 5, 6} , {7, 8, 9} }
>>m
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9

>>std.diag(m)
1    5    9    COL


>>m2=std.util.tomatrix{ {1, 2} , {4, 5} , {7, 8} }
>>m2
1 2
4 5
7 8

>>std.diag(m2)
1    5    COL
