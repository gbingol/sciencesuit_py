min

Uses minmax to find the minimum of an iteratable container.

min(container) → entry

min(Matrix, axes=) → Vector

>>v=std.util.tovector{3, 5, 2, 1, 7, 4}
>>v
3    5    2    1    7    4    COL

>>std.min(v)
1


>>m
