test_norm_ad

Performs Anderson-Darling test to test whether a sample of data came from a normal population.

Syntax:

test_norm_ad(cont)

argument cont must be Vector/Array.
Returns p-value and the test-statistic, namely the A2.

 

H0: The data follows the normal distribution.

 

Example:

>>pval, A2=std.test_norm_ad(vec)

>>pval
0.356325
>>A2
0.380373
