rnorm

Generates samples from normal distribution.
Returns a Vector or a number.

Syntax:

1) rnorm{n=, mean=0, sd=1}

2) rnorm(n, mean=0, sd=1)

 

n: Number of random numbers ← integer
mean: Mean of the distribution ← number
sd: Standard deviation ← number

Example:

>>v=std.rnorm(100)

>>std.mean(v)
0.00677

>>std.stdev(v)
0.964
