qf

Computes the lower tail quantile.
Returns number/Vector/Array.

Syntax:

1) qf{p=, df1=, df2=}

2) qf(p=, df1=, df2=)

 

p: Cumulative probability ← number/Vector/Array
df1, df2: Degrees of freedoms ← integer

Example:

>>std.pf{q=1, df1=3, df2=5}
0.535145

>>std.qf{p=0.535145, df1=3, df2=5}
0.999999
