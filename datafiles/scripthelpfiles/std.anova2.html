anova2

Performs two-factor analysis of variance test.
Returns a vector of p-values and an ANOVA table.

Syntax:

anova2 (yobs, x1, x2)

 

yobs: Response variable ← Vector/Array
x1, x2 Factor 1 and 2. ← Vector/Array

The order of p-values contained in the Vector is factor #1, factor #2 and interaction
