powfit

Fits the given dataset to equation of y=a*xn

Syntax:

powfit(x=, y=) → Vector
powfit{x=, y=} → Vector

where x and y are Vectors.
The function returns a vector containing the coefficients of the fitting.

 

Examples:

>>x=std.util.tovector{1,2,3,4}
>>y=1.2*x^2

>>std.powfit{x=x, y=y}
1.2     2     COL

corresponding to: y=1.2*x2
